Features

See below for some of Ombi's amazing features

Fully Integrated with DVR apps

Automatically adds requested content to Sonarr, Radarr, Sickrage, Headphones and more!

Comprehensive Notifications

Get notified when users request content through e-mail or your favourite notificaition app!

Customization

Comprehensive customization allows you to change the theme, site name and even add your own 'donate' link!

Fully Responsive

Your users can access Ombi through their phone, tablet or computer! Ombi will automatically change the UI to fit their screen.

Free to Use

Ombi is completely free and open source, with no ads! If you're happy with Ombi, please [donate] to support our development!

Content Issues Reporting

Users can report content issues, enabling a fully integrated Plex/Emby content management tool!
