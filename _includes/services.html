Features

See below for some of Ombi's amazing features

Fully Integrated

Automatically adds content to Sonarr, Radarr, Headphones and more

Comprehensive Notifications

Get notified when users request content through e-mail or your favourite notificaition app

Customization

Change the theme, site name, landing page message and even add your own 'donate' link

Fully Responsive

Users can access Ombi through their phone, tablet or computer - the UI adapts to their screen

Free & Open Source

Ombi is free and open source, with no ads! If you're happy with Ombi, please [donate] to support our development

Content Issues Reporting

Users can report content issues, enabling a fully integrated content management tool
